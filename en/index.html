Our programs

Cartoons starring your kids!

Cartoons starring your kids and their favourite animated characters in an immersive interactive performance at your home! Our programs are ideal for birthdays, home celebrations and even a special school occasion. We can easily transform any room, no matter the size, into a lively hub of fun, bringing joy wherever we are!

Here’s how it works:

Actors’ prep time is 15 min.

We provide a portable screen and projector to set the scene.

All we need from you is a socket, a fairly dark room (curtains on the windows are perfect), and a space of 3 by 5 metres.

The kids sit on the floor in front of the screen.

The program runs between 1-3 hours, with all the kids fully engaged and participating in the interactive experience!

About us:

The Tête de vedette project was brought to life by Polina Mikhailova and Arseny Epelbaum. 35 years ago, Arseny’s parents started their own puppet theatre and then passed down their artistry and extensive experience of working with children to us. With this knowledge and the benefits of technology, we create performances, parties, and special events for both adults and children around the globe!

FAQ:

What age group is the Tête de vedette program suitable for?

Our program is designed for children ages 6 to 12 and is sure to delight all children who love cartoons!

What type of children’s party can be organized at home?

We can bring a spectacular show right to your home! Our unique cartoon program allows the children to take centre stage, creating an unforgettable experience filled with fantastic videos for their special day.

What gift is ideal for kids aged 6-12?

Treat them to an amazing party featuring cartoons, entertainers and loads of fun. They’ll be involved and enjoy entertaining animated videos right in the comfort of your home!

Where do you travel?

We travel to Paris and surrounding areas, including Neuilly-sur-Seine, Saint-Cloud, Saint-Mandé, Boulogne-Billancourt, Maisons-Laffitte, Sèvres, Levallois-Perret, Val-de-Marne, and Yvelines. If your city’s not on the list, feel free to contact us and we're always happy to see if we can make it happen!

How far in advance should I start planning a home birthday party?

The earlier, the better. We can assist with special home party shows, animated video greetings, and more. Just get in touch!

Can the animator come in a princess, wizard, or pirate costume?

Yes, if you have a themed party, just let us know! Our animators will come in costumes of children’s favorite characters!

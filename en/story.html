How to Organize an Original Children Birthday Party at Home? The Tête de Vedette Program Will Help You!

Tête de vedette is an interactive show with video mapping for children aged 5 to 12.

Tête de vedette features cartoons where your children play the main roles!

Our programs are suitable for a birthday, a house party, or a school event.

How Does It Work?

An actor or actress comes to your home and, within 15 minutes, sets up a large screen and projector. The children sit in front of the screen.

In the middle of the screen, there is a hole where the child places their head. This way, the child is at the heart of the cartoon, and all the action happens around them.

For one program, we present 10 cartoons, each about 5 minutes long, which means that 10 guests can participate in the cartoons. If there are more children at the party, it’s not a problem. After the cartoons, dances with different characters begin on the same principle: each child can participate as a hero-dancer, and the others will mimic their movements!

The program lasts from 1 to 3 hours.

We produce all the cartoons ourselves, you won’t find them anywhere else.

The themes of the program can vary. For example, in the “Magical Tales” program, you will see cartoons like “Puss in Boots,” “The Three Musketeers,” “Cinderella,” and others.

We Care About Children’s Eye Safety! We have considered all the feedback from ophthalmologists in preparing our programs. The hole for the child’s face is not only cut out in the screen but also in the video, meaning the projector does not illuminate this area. Moreover, we do not use laser projectors, which can be dangerous for the eyes. Our projectors have ordinary lamps, making them safe. Do not try to replicate our techniques with your equipment; it’s dangerous for the eyes!

What Do You Need for This?

Any room with a free space of 3 by 5 meters is suitable for us. We can even adapt to a small room, and the furniture is not an obstacle! You will need to turn off the lights and draw the curtains, but complete darkness is not necessary. We need an ordinary electrical outlet.

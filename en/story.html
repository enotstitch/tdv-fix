Organize a Unique Children’s Birthday Party at Home with Tête de Vedette!

Tête de vedette is an interactive show with video mapping designed for kids aged 6 to 12.

Our program immerses your youngsters in a world of cartoons where they become the stars.

Perfect for birthdays, home parties, or school events, as we bring the magic right to your doorstep.

How It Works:

An actor or actress arrives at your home and sets up a large screen and projector in just 15 minutes.

The kids gather in front of the screen with a special cut-out for each child to place their head, making kids the centre of the cartoon action!

The entire program is between 1 to 3 hours.

WOur cartoons are truly one-of-a-kind and showcase a variety of exciting themes! For instance, our "Magical Tales" collection brings to life beloved stories like "Puss in Boots," "The Three Musketeers," and "Cinderella," ensuring every child’s favourite adventure is just a moment away!

We prioritise your child's eye safety.

Our programs are crafted with feedback from opticians.

What You’ll Need:

We only require a space of 3 by 5 metres. A little rearranging of furniture is no problem. Just dim the lights and draw the curtains (complete darkness isn’t necessary), and make sure you have access to a standard electrical outlet.

Let us help you create magical memories that your child will cherish forever!
